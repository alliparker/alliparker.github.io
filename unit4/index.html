Southern Hospitality
in the Windy City

Southern hospitality is associated with welcoming smiles, a conversation
peppered with "ya'lls", and the occasional "bless your heart". It is our goal
to bring a little taste of the South to the blustery Midwest. Our unique,
handcrafted cocktails and down-home classic Southern dishes make
Kitchen Bistro & Grill the perfect place to relax with friends and family.
