"Unlock the Value" Campaign

Developing a vision is closely linked with the ability to manage change.

In order to help IT achieve its vision, we are focusing our innovation efforts where we can have the greatest impact. As part of the Idea Lab’s “Unlock the Value” campaign, we are looking for the most compelling and creative ideas that help the organization achieve four strategic goals: Empowering our employees, empowering the enterprise, driving innovation and securing the Groupe.

Innovation Contest!

On December 1, we will begin accepting idea submissions for the “Unlock the Value” campaign. Your idea must help achieve IT's vision and be closely linked to one of the goals listed above.

So I've submitted an idea, now what?

The three-month campaign is structured around specific milestones, including the “Lion’s Den”, a collaborative session in which selected finalists can pitch fully fleshed out ideas to the Idea Lab team.
