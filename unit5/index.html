MY STORY

I’m a twenty-something young professional living in the Windy City, obsessed with pop culture, EDM, sweet tea, and all things tech. I'm a southern girl at heart – born and raised in North Carolina and a Tar Heel born and bred!

I spent the first five years post-college navigating the waters of our nation’s capital, working in the IT government sector as a communications specialist for a Senate-confirmed CIO. While living in DC, I also consulted for a large non-profit in the area, providing public relations services to several charitable organizations.

After four [longgg] years in government, I decided to take the plunge into private sector and move to a totally different city. Who knows, maybe the city of politics just wasn’t my cup of tea. Without thinking twice, I chose Chicago. Whenever I visited the city over the years I just felt different…maybe it was the view of the skyline flying into O’Hare or the hustle and bustle of Michigan Avenue.

I began applying for jobs during the summer of 2012 and voila! I was hired as a communications manager for a company that provides IT solutions to a global advertising conglomerate. My responsibilities include drafting technical communications and providing content strategy for our employees and clients in the US, Canada and Latin America.

My guilty pleasures include Avicii, dark nail polish, and Revenge on Netflix. So here I am – trying to make myself at home in my new city and rough out my first Chicago winter! Thanks for visiting and ya’ll come back now ya hear?
